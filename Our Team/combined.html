Albert Mihai Marin

Physics Team Member

I’m Albert, a 16-year-old student from Romania and former IJSO participant. When I first started studying for physics competitions, I never imagined I will qualify for something as big as the International Junior Science Olympiad (IJSO)—let alone win a bronze medal! I thought I wasn't capable of reaching this far.It was an incredible experience that taught me so much and motivated me to keep learning and growing.
Now, I’m super excited to be a coordinator for this IJSO preparation platform. I know how overwhelming things can feel at the beginning, but I also know how rewarding the journey can be. My goal is to support and inspire other students like you who are curious, motivated, and ready to take on the challenge. Let’s learn together, help each other out, and have some fun along the way!(trust me there is a lot of fun at ijso
