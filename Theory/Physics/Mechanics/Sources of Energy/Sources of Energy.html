Sources of Energy

Energy sources are materials or processes from which energy can be obtained for use in various applications, such as electricity generation, transportation, and industrial activities.

Types of Energy Sources

Energy sources can be categorized into two:

  • Renewable Sources
  • Non-Renewable Sources
